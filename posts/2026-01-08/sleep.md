Rest is the new luxury. From neuro-acoustic pillows to subscription-based REM optimization, the sleep tech industry is projected to hit $500 billion by 2030. 

As productivity demands increase, the value of a single hour of deep sleep has never been higher. However, critics argue that by turning rest into a quantifiable commodity, we are further eroding the natural boundaries between our working lives and our biological needs.

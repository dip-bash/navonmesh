Fast fashion is being replaced by "Living Garments." New startups are using fungal mycelium and algae-based fibers to create clothing that is not only biodegradable but actively sequesters carbon while you wear it. 

These textiles are self-healing and breathable in ways synthetic fibers could never achieve. The fashion industry, once a primary polluter, is slowly transforming into a massive carbon-capture engine.

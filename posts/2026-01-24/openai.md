category: "FINANCE"
readTime: "1 min read"

---

Sam Altman is negotiating a massive $50 billion funding round with Middle Eastern sovereign wealth funds, including the UAE’s MGX and Saudi Arabia’s PIF. The capital, equivalent to the GDP of entire nations, is earmarked for a $300 billion infrastructure deal with Oracle to build specialized AI data centers. This shift signals that AGI development has graduated from a venture capital asset to a geopolitical necessity. However, the unprecedented scale of foreign investment into dual-use technology will likely trigger intense CFIUS regulatory scrutiny.

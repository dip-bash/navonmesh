Vibe Coding is the new era of software engineering, according to Google AI lead Logan Kilpatrick. With tools like Gemini writing up to 90% of boilerplate code, the engineer’s role is shifting from syntax generation to "system orchestration". While this lowers the barrier for non-technical experts, it sparks concerns regarding the fragility of codebases built on "blind trust". Competition for leadership remains fierce; Google recently poached a Microsoft Corporate Vice President to bolster its AI divisions following its strategic partnership win with Apple.

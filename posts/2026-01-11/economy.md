The global market is pivoting toward a demographic reality we've long ignored. The 'Silver Peak' represents trillions in untapped economic potential as people live longer, healthier lives. 

Corporations are re-evaluating workforce policies, product design, and marketing strategies to cater to a demographic that refuses to 'retire' in the traditional sense. This shift promises to redefine our concept of age, productivity, and the very structure of the middle class.

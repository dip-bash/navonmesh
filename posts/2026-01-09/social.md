The hyper-engagement model is dying. In its place, "Quiet Webs"—private, encrypted, and small-scale digital circles—are becoming the preferred way for Gen Z and Alpha to communicate. 

The desire for algorithm-free interaction is driving a massive exodus from legacy platforms. People are trading "likes" for "depth," choosing platforms where the goal isn't to be seen by the world, but to be understood by a few.
